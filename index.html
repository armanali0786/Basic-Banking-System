The Spark Bank is a leading private sector bank in India. The Bank’s consolidated total assets stood at Rs. 8.76 trillion at September 30, 2020. The Spark Bank currently has a network of 5,00 branches and 5,158 ATMs across India.

The Spark Bank offers a wide range of banking products and financial services to corporate and retail customers through a variety of delivery channels and through its group companies.

The Spark Bank is deeply engaged in human and economic development at the national level. The Bank works closely with World Bank Foundation across diverse sectors and programs.
